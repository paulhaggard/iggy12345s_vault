Supervised Learning:
	Classification:
	- The output is between 0 and 1
	Regression:
	- There is a continuous output for the input samples.
		* Like how many items are going to be sold in the next month
		
Unsupervised Learning:
	Clustering:
		- We have a bunch of data, but don't necessarily know what the right answer is
		- Can also be used to cluster 'like' data together.
	Cocktail Party Problem:
		- Bunch of people talking at the same time
		- You want to be able to listen to everybody individually
